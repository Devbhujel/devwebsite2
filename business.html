Hotel Raniban Arcade

Raniban Arcade is an ornate, neo-classically structured deluxe-premium hotel, located in the heart of nature, just beside the legendary Queen Forest (aka Raniban). The hotel is ideally located a mere stone’s throw away from Fewa Lake. We pride ourselves in being eco-friendly and offer a comprehensive hospitality package, catered to suit both in-bound and out-bound tourists. Our objective is to manage the complexities of travel and accommodation, whilst providing our guests with the maximum amount of free quality time to spend with their friends & family.
